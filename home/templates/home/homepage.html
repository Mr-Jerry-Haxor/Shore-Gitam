About SHORe Fest

Welcome to SHORE Fest - GITAM University's Annual Fest An otherworldly experience with a kaleidoscope of cultural delicacies laced with music, art, food, sports and everything in between. With a rich history from years' worth of people before us, SHORE 2025 is a hot pot of art and athleticism. The beautiful yet hustling shores of Vizag inspire and embody the spirit of Shore, just as the calming briny breezes and the crashing waves illustrate the youthful energy of Gitamites.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SHORE Fest?

SHORE Fest is GITAM University's annual cultural festival, featuring a diverse range of events including music performances, art exhibitions, sports tournaments, and more.

When and where is SHORE Fest 2025 taking place?

SHORE Fest 2025 is scheduled for January 3-5, 2025, at GITAM University campus in Visakhapatnam, India.

How can I register for SHORE Fest?

You can register for SHORE Fest by clicking the 'Get Your Passes' button on our website. Follow the instructions to complete your registration and secure your spot at the festival.

Are there any age restrictions for attending SHORE Fest?

SHORE Fest is primarily designed for college students and young adults. However, specific age restrictions may apply to certain events. Please check the details of individual events for any age-related requirements.

Can non-GITAM students attend SHORE Fest?

Yes, SHORE Fest is open to students from other colleges and universities as well. We welcome participants from all over to join in the celebration!
