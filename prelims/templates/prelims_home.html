Dance Competition Prelims

DC_PRELIMS Guidelines:

Team Limit: Each team can have 12 to 15 members.

Time Limit: Performances should not exceed more than 10 minutes. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.

Audio Submission Deadline: January 7th, 2024

Audio and Background Image Submissions: Upload your team's audio in the Shore website, under the PRELIMS section.

Guidelines:

  • Theme: It is mandatory to unite any of the Indian dance forms with any other global style.
  • Live Music: Not allowed.
  • Props: Utilized props should not disturb the environment.
  • Accessories, Props, and Costumes: Participants need to arrange these items.
  • Audio Content: Should not demean or discriminate against any community.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Adherence to the stipulated theme.
  2. Choreography.
  3. Stage utilization.
  4. Expressions.
  5. Synchronization.

Music Competition Prelims

Music Competition Guidelines

Team Limit:

Each band can have at most 6 participants.

Time Limit:

Should not exceed more than 8 minutes for each performance. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.

Background Image Submission:

Upload background images required for the performance in the Shore website, under the PRELIMS section.

Guidelines:

  1. Theme: It is mandatory to unite any of the Indian dance forms with any other global style.
  2. A band of aid or a solo act both are allowed as they sing and play at least one instrument.
  3. Any distasteful behavior or damage to the surrounding equipment or supplies will result in disqualification.
  4. Only the keyboard part of the synthesizer is allowed. (Rhythm part is not allowed.)
  5. The Shore team will not be held responsible for the failure of instruments. Participants are advised to ensure the proper functioning of their own equipment.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Melodic Creativity: Originality in composition.
  2. Harmony: Effective blending of vocal and instrumental elements.
  3. Instrumental Skill: Proficiency in playing at least one instrument.
  4. Vocal Quality: Clarity, pitch accuracy, and expression.
  5. Fusion Integration: Seamless blending of diverse elements.

Glam Voyage Prelims

Fashion Competition Guidelines

Time Limit:

5 Minutes

Background music Submission Deadline:

January 7th, 2024

Background music Submission:

Upload your team's audios on the Shore website, under the PRELIMS section.

Guidelines:

  1. Designers are encouraged to incorporate garbage bags into their outfit, showcasing creativity and resourcefulness.
  2. Using any substance (or prop) that poses danger or might alter the stage in any way for the next team is not allowed (e.g., spilling of liquid that will affect the dryness).
  3. The teams will be expected to restore the stage to its original condition within the set time limit in case any prop or substance that causes temporary changes is used.
  4. Vulgarity is not allowed.
  5. Obscenity will be left to judges' discretion, and their decision will be final and binding in case any part of the performance is found objectionable.
  6. Participants should provide a brief explanation of how they meet the judging criteria during the preliminary round.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Theme Relevance.
  2. Creativity and Innovation.
  3. Execution of Design.
  4. Creative utilization of waste materials.
  5. Aesthetic - The visual appeal of the outfit.
  6. Prop Utilization - If props are used, their integration into the performance without disrupting the overall flow.
  7. A focus on maintaining originality in design will be assessed during the preliminary round.
  8. Quality of final product.

Nukkad Naatak Prelims

Guidelines:

Team Limit:

6-15 members excluding 2 instrumentalists (optional)

Time Limit:

10 minutes

Guidelines:

  1. Usage of fire, water, or smoke is not allowed.
  2. Gulaal or any props, if used, must be cleared off by the participants after their performance within the allotted time.
  3. Any instance of vulgarity or disturbing content of any order will lead to a forceful halt of performance and immediate disqualification.
  4. Only live music is allowed. Teams will have to bring their own instruments.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Content, Theme, and Message.
  2. Performance, Direction, and Gimmicks.
  3. Interaction and Improvisation.
  4. Space Utilization

Debate Prelims

Debate Competition Guidelines:

Team Limit:

4 to 6 people per team will be allowed

5 min for opening statement and 5 for closing statement. A team will only get 10 more minutes on one go.

Guidelines:

  1. The theme for the event will be announced directly at the venue.
  2. All teams will be given 10 minutes after the announcement to use any and all resources while being at the venue to search and find out the theme of the debate.
  3. The debate will follow the general rules of the British Parliamentary Debate rules.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Each team will be given a time limit of 2 minutes on turn.
  2. Teams should engage in a fair and respectful debate.
  3. Any vulgarity will not be tolerated and be disqualified.
  4. Members of the opposing team may offer points of information (POIs) during the speech.
  5. The teams will be judged based on fluency, negotiation skills and etiquette.

Canvas Prelims

Canvas Guidelines:

Team Limit:

Individual event

Time Limit:

2 hours

Guidelines:

    Theme: The theme for the prelims will be announced on spot at the venue.

  1. Each participant will have to get all the resources for their art projects on their end(including sheets to draw/paint)
  2. Participants will not be allowed to use any references during or after the announcement of the theme.
  3. Minimum size of the canvas: 22 inches by 28 inches(Chart paper)
  4. Participants can use any type of art styles and forms (No Limits)
  5. Each participant will have to talk about their topics and describe it for about a minute.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Participants will be judged based on their creativity and how their pieces adhere to the theme.
  2. They will also be judged based on the presentation of their topics after the showcase.
  3. Participants will be judged on the aesthetic of their events.
  4. Any sense of vulgarity will not be tolerated, and the participants will be disqualified from the prelims
  5. Final discretion will be held by the judges.

Cooking Prelims

Master chef Guidelines:

Team Limit:

Individual event

Time Limit:

Each participant is to make a dish in under 1 hour.

Guidelines:

  1. Each participant will have to make a dish of their choice in the comfort of their preferred location.
  2. They will have to take a video of the entire process of them cooking, video does not have to be 1 hour long.
  3. The committee will specify a certain ingredient which will have to be included in any form of their dish.
  4. Regular food dishes are not encouraged.
  5. All the ingredients and methods must be specified in a document or in the video itself.

Judging Criteria:

  1. Participants will be judged based on their efficiency, how they handle the work load.
  2. They will also be judged on their hygiene and the dish preparation.
  3. They will be assessed on their creativity, originality, and use of the specific ingredient in their dish.
  4. The clarity of the video and the instructions that are provided by the participant will also be considered by the judging panel.
  5. The final presentation will also play a major role in the assessments.
